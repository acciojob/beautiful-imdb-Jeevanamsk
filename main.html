The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring is a 2001 epic fantasy adventure film directed by Peter Jackson, based on the novel by J. R. R. Tolkien. It is the first installment in the Lord of the Rings trilogy, and follows hobbit Frodo Baggins as he and a fellowship of others set out on a quest to destroy the One Ring and defeat the evil Sauron. The film stars Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Viggo Mortensen, Sean Astin, and more.
